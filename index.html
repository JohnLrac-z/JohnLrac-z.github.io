STUDENT ACTIVITY CENTER

The student activity center features a cafe-style foyer for collaboration and socialization, connecting to a newly-established outdoor open space as dance and theater rehearsals and flexible meeting bays. Purposely built to host multiple campus programs, this 400-sqm facility also provides a new venue for university wide gatherings. This center offers an indoor facility's convenience, wherein other stakeholders' need for space celebrations and occasions is accommodated. This facility is a new step in bringing the academic, cultural, athletic departments, and the community closer together.
